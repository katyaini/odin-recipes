Try this healthy turmeric milk tea.

Ingredients

  • 1-inch piece of fresh turmeric root
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 inch ginger
  • cinnamon
  • pinch of ground black pepper
  • Honey or any preffered sweetner, Optional
  • 1 Tbsp Ghee

Steps

  1. Boil turmeric and other whole spices with water. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Strain the water
    3. Now boil strained water again, add milk and black pepper powder.
  3. Top with honey and ghee. Mix well and enjoy
